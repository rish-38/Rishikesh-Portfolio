Zomato Case Study

Zomato is one of the leading food delivery and restaurant discovery platforms. While the platform offers a vast range of features, certain UI/UX aspects could be improved for a more seamless user experience. This case study focuses on redesigning Zomato interface to enhance usability, visual appeal, and overall efficiency, addressing key pain points faced by users.

Sign In page

Inktober, the globally recognized art challenge, lacks an official sign-in page for participants to track their progress and engage with the community. To address this, I designed and developed a custom sign-in page using the official Inktober color palette and branding style, ensuring a seamless and visually cohesive user experience.
